Our Mission

To preserve and promote the rich tradition of Filipino lambanog while making it accessible to everyone through modern e-commerce. We believe in connecting people with authentic Filipino culture through our carefully curated selection of premium lambanog products.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for authentic Filipino spirits, Lam Shop has grown from a small local business to a trusted online destination for lambanog enthusiasts. We work directly with traditional distilleries to bring you the finest quality products while supporting local communities and preserving time-honored brewing techniques.
